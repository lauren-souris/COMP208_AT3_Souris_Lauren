Teaching Resources

Downloadable worksheets, templates, and checklists for Food Technology and Industrial Arts.

Overview

Every resource here has been classroom-tested and built with UDL, safety, and clear success criteria in mind. Use these directly or adapt them for your own classes. Preview each item below and download from the link provided.

Year 9 Food Fusion — E-Book / Assessment Task Scaffold

A structured student booklet guiding learners from brief → research → sketches → trial cook → final product. Includes nutrition analysis, costing, sensory evaluation, safety notes, and clear success criteria.

  • Use: multi-week portfolio assessment
  • Includes: criteria, checkpoints, sentence starters, reflection prompts

Food Fusion — Student Activity Template

Quick worksheet: students pick two cultural influences, name their dish, sketch ideas, and justify key ingredient swaps. Great as a warm-up, exit ticket, or pre-cook planning sheet.

  • Use: one-lesson ideation scaffold
  • Focus: culture, flavour pairing, justification
Download Template JPG

Food Photography — Setup Guide

Side-by-side example of a simple light tent setup and the resulting image. Ideal for teaching presentation skills, lighting basics, and composition for folios.

  • Use: demo slide or checklist for plating/lighting
  • Focus: composition, angles, natural light
Download Guide JPG

Design & Technology — Folio / Portfolio Template

Step-by-step folio scaffold from brief to evaluation. Includes brainstorms, sketches, materials, safety notes, test photos, feedback checkpoints, and a mini timeline.

  • Use: term-length project documentation
  • Focus: ideation → prototyping → reflection
Download Template JPG

SWMS — Safe Work Method Statement (Example)

Sample SWMS layout covering job steps, equipment, hazards, controls, and risk ratings. Use it as a reference or adapt it for class-based safety activities.

  • Use: WHS teaching reference
  • Focus: risk assessment, hazard control, documentation
