About Me

Pre-service TAS Teacher — Food Technology & Industrial Arts

Hi, I’m Lauren

I’m a TAS teacher-in-training with a love for Food Technology, Industrial Arts, and hands-on learning. Currently studying at the Australian Catholic University, I focus on designing lessons that help students create, problem-solve, and explore their ideas in safe, inclusive environments.

Outside the classroom, I run a disability support business, helping people of all abilities grow their confidence and independence. Whether in the kitchen, the workshop, or the community, I’m passionate about creating opportunities for learning and connection.

My Teaching Philosophy

“Great teaching begins with listening to learners.”
